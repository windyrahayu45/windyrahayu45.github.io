Portfolio

Most recent work

Benevesia Task Management System

A comprehensive web-based application developed for Benevesia to streamline and optimize internal employee operations. Built using the Laravel framework, the system manages employee attendance, task assignments, and performance reporting in an integrated platform.

Key features include daily check-in/check-out monitoring, task scheduling with progress tracking, and automated report generation to assist managers in evaluating team productivity. The application ensures data security and provides role-based access control to maintain operational integrity. This platform significantly enhances workflow efficiency, transparency, and accountability across departments within the company.

One Data Portal for Padang Panjang

A web-based application developed using the Laravel framework for the Government of Padang Panjang City. This platform serves as an integrated portal to publish sectoral data and statistical figures (Data Dalam Angka) in collaboration with Statistics Indonesia (BPS). The system is designed to centralize various datasets from different local government agencies, promoting data transparency, accessibility, and evidence-based decision making. It also provides interactive data visualizations and advanced filtering features to enable the public, researchers, and policymakers to easily explore and utilize regional data.

CCTV Padang Panjang

A web-based application developed using the Laravel framework that provides live streaming access to public CCTV cameras across the City of Padang Panjang. This platform enables residents, government officials, and security stakeholders to monitor real-time conditions throughout key areas in the city, supporting safety, situational awareness, and rapid response initiatives. It also promotes transparency and community engagement by allowing the public to observe traffic and public space activity directly through the web portal.

Demo

Kepo (Kepegawaian Online)

This project comprises the development of an integrated personnel services platform for the City Government of Solok, encompassing both a web-based application developed using PHP with the CodeIgniter framework and a dedicated Android mobile application. The system facilitates end-to-end personnel administration, including master employee data management, performance allowance (TPP) processing, leave and transfer management, attendance tracking, supervisory and grievance services, as well as an Electronic Official Correspondence System (Sinadine/e-Office) to streamline the handling of official documents and correspondence within the regional government.

Sipedas (PKK and Dasawisma Information System)

This project involves the development of an integrated information system designed to support the activities of the Family Welfare Movement (PKK) and Dasawisma groups at the community level. The solution includes both a web-based application and an Android mobile application that enable PKK cadres to efficiently collect, manage, and monitor citizen demographic data and household profiles within their respective neighborhoods. The system facilitates data-driven planning and reporting, assists in tracking community welfare programs, and enhances the effectiveness of local government initiatives by providing accurate and up-to-date population data.

Demo

PPDB (Penerimaan Peserta Didik Baru) for SD & SMP

A CodeIgniter-based web application that manages the complete admission process for primary (SD) and junior high (SMP) schools in Solok City, covering registration, selection, announcement, and re-enrollment. The system applies zoning-based selection and ranking, and integrates PKH beneficiary data and population records to ensure equitable and transparent student placement.

UMKM Manjua

An Android-based e-commerce application specifically developed for micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs/UMKM) in Solok City. This platform enables local entrepreneurs to showcase and sell their products online, helping to expand market reach, increase sales, and strengthen the regional economy. The application offers features for product listings, order management, and customer engagement, all designed to support the digital transformation of traditional businesses in the area.

Demo

SAKATO (SurAt KeterAngan Terbit Online)

A web-based application built with the CodeIgniter framework that serves as an online service portal for the residents of Solok City. Through this platform, citizens can submit requests for various official letters and certificates, track the progress of their applications in real-time, and receive documents that are digitally signed with an electronic signature. This system enhances the efficiency, accessibility, and transparency of public administrative services.

Demo

SANITASI (Sistem Aplikasi Manajemen Infrastruktur Terintegrasi)

A web-based Geographic Information System (GIS) application specifically developed for the Public Works and Spatial Planning Agency (PUPR) of Solok City. This system facilitates comprehensive data collection, visualization, and management of public infrastructure assets, enabling more effective planning, monitoring, and maintenance activities. By leveraging interactive maps and spatial analysis, the application supports data-driven decision-making to improve infrastructure development and service delivery across the city.

Solok City Integrated API Services

I served as the backend developer responsible for designing and implementing robust RESTful APIs that power various web and mobile applications within the Solok City Government ecosystem. These APIs enable seamless data exchange and integration across multiple platforms, supporting services such as population management, social welfare targeting, infrastructure GIS mapping, online document issuance, and more. The unified API architecture ensures data consistency, security, and scalability to meet the diverse operational needs of the city's digital transformation initiatives.
